They all want me

How to Play

This is a fun game where you are a character trying to find your school.

You move around using the arrow keys on your keyboard. Try to avoid the universities, which are the pictures you see like 'harvard.png', 'mit.png', etc. If any university catched you, you have to restart!

Your goal is to find your school, who is represented by the picture 'slps.png'. If you bump into your school, you win the game!

The game will keep running until you win. Have fun finding your school!
